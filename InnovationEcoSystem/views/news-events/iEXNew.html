VHA iEX Talks uses storytelling to highlight the ingenuity and power of innovation in creating real value and impact for VHA employees, academia, industry partners, and the Veteran community. Innovation Stories

“Innovation Stories” a series of three unique innovation presentations:

Innovator Experiences- in which frontline employees participating in VHA iNET’s Spark-Seed-Spread Innovation Investment and Accelerator Program tell you the story of their journey from employee to innovator.

Innovation Exhibition- in which Office of Healthcare Innovation and Learning and National Centers for Innovation to Impact staff present the progress of their initiatives and programs.

Innovation Demos- in which a selection of last year’s Breaking Boundaries Collaboration Challenge winners and their VHA iNET Innovation Specialist counterparts give a live demonstration on the progress of their collaborative innovations.

VHA Shark Tank


Read More

How does VHA reimagine Veteran healthcare? By empowering an ecosystem of employees to harness the power of innovation and bring submerged solutions to life. VHA Shark Tank Competition promotes VHA staff and front-line employees to make waves across VHA by breaking boundaries to challenging healthcare issues. The competition provides a platform for leadership exposure and illuminates those who are innovating and forging the future of Veteran healthcare. The eighth cycle of the competition forecasts a swell of passionate innovators hungry to change and save Veteran lives as Medical Center and Veterans Integrated Services Network (VISN) Director Sharks fish for new practices. This seasons fin-tastic finalists will pitch their shorebreaking solutions live and compete for a chance to cast their unique innovations to other VHA facilities during the 2022 VHA Shark Tank Competition at the VHA Innovation Experience (iEX) in October!

VA Intrapreneur Product Marketplace Day


Read More

As many of you know from experience, the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) Veterans Health Administration (VHA) has a long history driving innovation forward. Integral to the past and critical to the future of innovation, lies VHA’s frontline employees. Within VHA, the Innovators Network (iNET) takes a ground-up approach by using the first-hand knowledge of frontline employees to identify some of VA’s greatest challenges and develop both Veteran- and employee-centric solutions.

To showcase the impactful innovative work of VHA frontline employees, the VHA Office of Healthcare Innovation and Learning is hosting a special invite-only event during the annual VHA Innovation Experience (iEX); the first VA Intrapreneur Product Marketplace Day.

During this face-to-face industry day-like event, you will hear from 12 frontline employee innovators throughout the country who have designed an innovative product ready for manufacturing or commercialization (with the ability to license) and see their completed product design or minimally viable product.
